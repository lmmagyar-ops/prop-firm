## Universal Rules (All Tiers)

### Trading Markets
- **Allowed**: Polymarket and Kalshi prediction markets only
- **Markets**: Politics, sports, economics, crypto, entertainment, and all other Polymarket and Kalshi categories
- **Leverage**: None - all positions are 1:1 to the markets

### Evaluation Rules
1. **Profit Target**: Hit your tier's profit target (10-12% depending on account size)
2. **Drawdown Limit**: Never exceed your tier's drawdown limit (varies by tier: 8-10%)
3. **Daily Loss Limit**: Cannot lose more than your tier's daily limit in a single trading day (varies by tier: 4-5%)
4. **Minimum Days**: Must trade at least 5 days to pass evaluation
5. **No Time Limit**: Take as long as you need to hit your target (60 day suggested maximum)
6. **Minimum Trade Size**: $10 per trade

### What Happens When You Pass?
1. You get a funded account with the same capital size
2. Keep trading with our capital
3. Bi-weekly payouts (every 14 days) - first month is 30-day payout cycle
4. 80% profit split (or 90% if you purchased the upgrade)
5. Your evaluation fee is refunded with your first payout

### What Happens If You Hit Your Drawdown?
1. Your evaluation ends immediately
2. Learn from it and start a new challenge when ready
3. Your evaluation fee is non-refundable if you fail
4. Many successful traders failed 2-3 times before getting funded
